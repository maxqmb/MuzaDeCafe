Our story

A Muse
in Every Cup

Muza de Cafe opened its doors on October 9, 2022, inspired by the Polish word for "muse" — symbolizing the source of inspiration that drives us to create a unique and exceptional coffee experience.

Our founders, Engr. Ruben Arciga and Lapurisima Arciga, are not only passionate about coffee but also carry a rich family legacy. Their daughter, a beauty queen, embodies the same spirit of excellence and dedication that we strive to bring to every cup we serve.
